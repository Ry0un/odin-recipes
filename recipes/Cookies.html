Cookies Recipe

Description:

Chocolate chip cookies are a beloved treat, famous for their soft, chewy centers and crispy edges.
Loaded with semi-sweet chocolate chips, they offer a delightful mix of sweet and slightly salty flavors in every bite.
These cookies are perfect for sharing or enjoying with a glass of milk.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat together softened butter, sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then mix in the vanilla extract.
  6. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until combined.
  7. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  8. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden but centers are still soft.
  10. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
