Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description:

A chocolate cake is a delightful treat known for its rich, moist texture and deep chocolate flavor.
Made with cocoa powder and melted chocolate, it's often layered and topped with smooth chocolate frosting or ganache.
Each bite is a sweet, chocolatey indulgence that's perfect for any celebration or simply as a comforting dessert.

Ingredients:

Cake:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Boiling water

Frosting:

  • Unsalted butter
  • Cocoa powder
  • Powdered sugar
  • Milk or cream
  • Vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans or line them with parchment paper.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. In another bowl, beat the eggs, then add milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Stir in boiling water (or hot coffee) until the batter is smooth.
  7. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then turn them out onto wire racks to cool completely.
  10. For the frosting, beat together softened butter, cocoa powder, and powdered sugar. Add milk and vanilla extract until smooth and spreadable.
  11. Frost and decorate the cooled cakes as desired.
