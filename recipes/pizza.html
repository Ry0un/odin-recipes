Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza Margherita is a timeless Italian favorite that's all about fresh, simple ingredients. The thin, crispy crust is topped with a rich tomato sauce made from juicy tomatoes and fragrant basil.
A generous layer of creamy mozzarella melts into a gooey, delicious blanket, perfectly balancing the tangy sauce.
After baking, a handful of fresh basil leaves is added for a burst of vibrant flavor.
Every bite captures the essence of Italian cooking, celebrating the beauty of less being more.

Ingredients:

Dough:

  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Hot Water
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Tomato Sauce:

  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Dried Oregano
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Toppings:

  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Bazil Leaves
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

To make the Dough:

  1. Mix yeast and sugar in warm water, then let it foam.
  2. Combine flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise until it doubles in size.

To make the sauce:

  1. Saut√© minced garlic in olive oil, then add crushed tomatoes, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Simmer for 15-20 minutes and season with salt and pepper.

Assembling

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting.
  2. Roll out the dough and spread on tomato sauce.
  3. Top with slices of mozzarella.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Serving:

  1. Top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil. Slice and enjoy!
